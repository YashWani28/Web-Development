Only Delhi domiciled candidates belonging from SC/ ST/ OBC/ Minorities can apply for this scheme.,Fee Reimbursement scheme is available for students of class 1st to class 12th studying in recognized/affiliated with Dept. of Education, Govt. of National Capital Territory (NCT) of Delhi /Municipal local bodies of Delhi.,In order to apply for this scheme, students must not be the recipient of any other scholarship.,The family income of the students must not exceed INR 2 lacs.
